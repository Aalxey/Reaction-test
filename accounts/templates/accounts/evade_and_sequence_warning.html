🎮 Evade & Sequence

A challenging dual-task game that tests your focus, reaction time, and multitasking abilities

🎯 What You'll Be Doing

You'll be doing two things at the same time - like patting your head and rubbing your tummy! Don't worry, it starts easy and gets harder as you get better.

💡 Pro Tip

Focus on one task at a time - start with the numbers, then check the minimap, then back to numbers. You'll get the hang of it quickly!

📊 Task 1: Click Numbers

Simple: Click numbers 1, 2, 3, 4, 5 in order

  • Numbers 1-5 appear on screen
  • Click them in order: 1 → 2 → 3 → 4 → 5
  • The next number glows yellow
  • Complete the set for bonus points!

⚡ Task 2: Watch for Enemies

Simple: Click EVADE when red dot gets close

  • Watch the small map in top-left corner
  • Red dot moves toward you
  • When it enters the blue circle, click EVADE
  • If it reaches you, game over!

🏆 How You Score Points

Here's how you earn points and what to avoid:

✓ Right number: +10 points
★ Complete 1-5 set: +50 bonus
✗ Wrong number: +1 miss
💀 3 misses = Game Over

📱 Before You Start

⚠️ Important!

Turn your phone sideways (landscape mode) - the game won't work properly in portrait mode!

🌐 Best Browser

Use Google Chrome for the smoothest experience

📋 Quick Checklist

  • ✅ Phone turned sideways
  • ✅ Using Chrome browser
  • ✅ Comfortable position
  • ✅ Good lighting
  • ✅ Ready to have fun!

🎮 Don't Stress! The game starts easy and gets harder as you improve. Everyone struggles at first - that's normal! Just have fun and do your best.
