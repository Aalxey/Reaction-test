🎮 Evade & Sequence

A challenging dual-task game that tests your focus, reaction time, and multitasking abilities

🎯 Game Overview

This is a dual-task challenge that requires you to simultaneously perform two different tasks: clicking numbers in sequence and evading an approaching enemy. The game adapts to your skill level, starting easy but becoming progressively more challenging as you improve.

📊 Sequence Task

  • Numbers 1-5 spawn randomly on screen
  • Click them in ascending order: 1→2→3→4→5
  • Yellow highlight shows the next number to click
  • Complete full sequences for bonus points
  • Wrong numbers or timeouts count as misses

⚡ Evasion Task

  • Red enemy dot moves toward you on the minimap
  • Watch the minimap in the top-left corner
  • When enemy enters the blue detection range
  • Click the EVADE button to teleport enemy away
  • If enemy reaches you, it is game over!

🏆 Scoring System

✓ Correct number: +10 points
★ Complete sequence: +50 bonus
✗ Wrong number: +1 miss
💀 3 misses = Game Over

⚠️ Important Preparation

🌐 Browser Recommendation

Use Google Chrome browser for the best experience and full feature support

📱 Landscape Mode Required

This game is designed for LANDSCAPE mode only for optimal gameplay!

📋 Before Starting Checklist

  • ✅ Use Google Chrome browser
  • ✅ Rotate your device to landscape mode
  • ✅ Find a comfortable position
  • ✅ Ensure you have good lighting
  • ✅ Minimize distractions
  • ✅ Get ready to focus intensely!

🧠 Challenge Level: This game adapts to your skill level. It starts easy but gets progressively more challenging as you improve. Do not worry if it feels difficult at first - practice makes perfect!
